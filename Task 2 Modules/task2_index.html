Overview of Requirements

This task develops safe, accurate tool selection and use across hand tools and power tools.

  • MEM18001: Use hand tools.
  • MEM18002: Use power tools/hand held operations.

Evidence collection: Section 1 knowledge quizzes (Parts A, B, C) and Section 2 direct observation (Observations 1-4).

Training and Assessment Schedule

Delivery period: 40 hours scheduled in Term 1–3 (Year 11, Preliminary).

Assessment timing: Assessments can occur at any point after relevant teaching and learning has occurred and when the student and trainer agree readiness.

Continuation note: Task 2 can continue during Task 4 and Task 5 if students have not yet been deemed Achieved.
