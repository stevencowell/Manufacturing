Overview of Requirements

Task 3 prepares students to interpret job requirements, perform machining computations, use digital tools, and complete machine setup/operation to specification.

  • MEM12024: Perform computations.
  • MEM16008: Interact with computer technology.
  • MEM07032: Use workshop machines for basic operations.

Assessment structure: Section 1 Written Questioning, Section 2 Product Based Methods, and Section 3 Direct Observation.

Evidence Preparation Focus

Learning materials in this task are structured to prepare students for official assessor package evidence:

  • Computation and machine-operations knowledge checks
  • Drill speed graphing and data interpretation
  • CAD outputs (3D + orthographic views to AS1100 expectations)
  • Computer-generated material list with formulas and cost graph
  • Direct-observation readiness for planning and safe operation criteria
