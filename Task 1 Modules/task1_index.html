Overview of Requirements

This task builds foundation capability for working in a manufacturing environment across three units.

  • MEM13015: Work safely and effectively in manufacturing and engineering.
  • MEM16006: Organise and communicate information.
  • MEM11011: Undertake manual handling.

Evidence for competency is collected through knowledge checks, structured activities, and practical observation. You are expected to show safe work habits, correct workplace communication, and quality-focused task planning.

Workshop Safety

Know your tools, follow SOPs, and wear the right PPE.

Personal Protective Equipment

Correct PPE is required before you start any task.

Planning and Communication

Use accurate measurement and clear instructions before you start.
